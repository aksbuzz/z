easy pb oatmeal cookies

1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 1/4 cup quick-cooking oats
1/2 tsp baking soda

mix pb & sugar 'til fluffy, then egg, then oats & baking soda
bake around 8 minutes at 350
